# Acetaminophen (Paracetamol)

## Description
Acetaminophen is a commonly used medication for pain relief and fever reduction.

## Indications
- Fever
- Headache
- Mild to moderate pain

## Dosage
- Adults: 500–1000 mg every 4–6 hours (max 4000 mg/day)

## Side Effects
- Rare liver damage (especially overdose)
- Nausea

## Precautions
- Avoid alcohol consumption
- Do not exceed recommended dose

## When to Seek Medical Care
- Signs of overdose
- Yellowing of skin (liver issues)

## Disclaimer
Use only as directed. Consult a doctor if unsure.
