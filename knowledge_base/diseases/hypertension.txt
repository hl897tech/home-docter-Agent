# Hypertension

## Definition
Hypertension is a chronic condition where blood pressure is consistently elevated (≥130/80 mmHg).

## Causes
- Genetics
- Poor diet (high salt intake)
- Lack of physical activity
- Stress
- Obesity

## Symptoms
- Often asymptomatic
- Headaches
- Dizziness

## What You Can Do
- Reduce salt intake
- Exercise regularly
- Maintain healthy weight
- Monitor blood pressure

## When to Seek Medical Care
- Blood pressure consistently ≥140/90 mmHg
- Chest pain
- Vision changes
- Severe headache

## Disclaimer
Always consult a healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment.
