# Choking First Aid

## Definition
Choking occurs when an object blocks the airway, preventing normal breathing.

## Signs
- Inability to speak
- Clutching throat
- Cyanosis (bluish lips)
- Silent cough

## What You Should Do
- Encourage coughing
- Perform Heimlich maneuver
- Call emergency services if severe

## When It Is an Emergency
- Person becomes unconscious
- Cannot breathe or speak

## Disclaimer
Seek immediate emergency assistance in life-threatening situations.
