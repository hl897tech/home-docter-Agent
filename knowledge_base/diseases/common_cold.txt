# Common Cold

## Definition
The common cold is a viral infection of the upper respiratory tract, usually mild and self-limiting.

## Causes
- Rhinoviruses (most common)
- Coronaviruses
- Respiratory viruses

## Symptoms
- Runny nose
- Sneezing
- Sore throat
- Mild fever
- Cough

## What You Can Do
- Get plenty of rest
- Stay hydrated
- Use saline nasal spray
- Take over-the-counter medications

## When to Seek Medical Care
- Symptoms lasting more than 10 days
- High fever
- Difficulty breathing
- Worsening symptoms
