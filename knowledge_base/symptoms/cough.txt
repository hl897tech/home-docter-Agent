# Cough

## Definition
Cough is a reflex action that helps clear the airways of irritants, mucus, or foreign particles.

## Common Causes
- Common cold
- Bronchitis
- Asthma
- Allergies
- Pneumonia

## Symptoms
- Dry or productive cough
- Throat irritation
- Chest discomfort

## What You Can Do
- Drink warm fluids
- Use a humidifier
- Take honey (for age >1 year)
- Avoid smoke or pollutants

## When to Seek Medical Care
- Cough lasting more than 2 weeks
- Coughing up blood
- Shortness of breath
- High fever with cough

## Disclaimer
Consult a healthcare professional if symptoms are severe or persistent.
