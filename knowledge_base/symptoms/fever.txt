# Fever

## Definition
Fever is a temporary increase in body temperature, typically above 37.3°C (99.1°F), often due to an underlying illness.

## Common Causes
- Viral infections (e.g., flu, cold)
- Bacterial infections
- Inflammatory conditions
- Heat exhaustion

## Symptoms
- Elevated body temperature
- Sweating or chills
- Headache
- Fatigue

## What You Can Do
- Stay hydrated
- Rest
- Use antipyretics (e.g., acetaminophen)
- Keep room well ventilated

## When to Seek Medical Care
- Temperature ≥ 39.5°C (103°F)
- Fever lasting more than 3 days
- Seizures or confusion
- Infants under 3 months with fever
